# New Light Rise — One‑Page Pitch

## The Problem
Student‑athletes juggle recruiting, training, and content across scattered tools. Coaches and recruiters lack a unified, credible view of performance and academic fit.

## The Solution
**New Light Rise** is the athlete‑first platform that unifies highlights, live streams, training, and college fit into one clean experience.

## Core Features
- **College Fit**: Real data‑driven recommendations with PDF reports for recruiters.
- **Profile Dashboard**: One profile powering all evaluations and outreach.
- **Training Requests**: Match athletes with local trainers based on goals + budget.
- **Coach Evaluations**: Structured feedback and ratings.
- **Video & Live**: Share highlight links and live streams in minutes.
- **Editing Plans**: Save cut ranges and notes for quick highlight creation.

## Why It Wins
- **Unified**: One platform for athletes, coaches, and recruiters.
- **Credible**: Data‑backed insights + structured evaluations.
- **Fast**: Simple workflows designed for real‑world recruiting timelines.

## Traction (Prototype)
- Working MVP with Google sign‑in and real data integration.
- PDF export for college‑fit reports.
- Live stream embedding and training matching.

## Next Step
Pilot with 2–3 local high schools / clubs and recruit feedback from coaches and college scouts.
