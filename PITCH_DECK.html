New Light Rise — One‑Page Pitch

Athlete‑first platform for recruiting, training, and content.

The Problem

Student‑athletes juggle recruiting, training, and content across scattered tools. Coaches and recruiters lack a unified, credible view of performance and academic fit.

The Solution

New Light Rise unifies highlights, live streams, training, and college fit into one clean experience.

Core Features

College Fit + PDF reports
Profile Dashboard
Training Requests + Matching
Coach Evaluations
Video + Live Streams
Editing Plans

Why It Wins

  • Unified for athletes, coaches, and recruiters
  • Data‑backed evaluations
  • Fast workflows for recruiting timelines

Prototype Traction

Working MVP with Google sign‑in, data‑driven college fit, training matching, live streams, and PDF reporting.

Next Step
